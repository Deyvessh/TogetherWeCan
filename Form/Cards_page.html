    Collabrative efforts done by active NGOs in India


    HelpAge India

    HelpAge India is a leading charity in India working with and for disadvantaged elderly for nearly 4 decades. There are an estimated 104 million elderly in India. It works hand-in-hand with Senior Citizens Associations understanding elder needs working with and for them.

    Smile Foundation

    Smile Foundation is to empower underprivileged children through relevant education, innovative healthcare and market-focused livelihood programmes to practice and promote good governance. also to sensitize privileged children to promote Civic Driven Change.

    Nanhi Kali

    Project Nanhi Kali is working with 19 NGO partners including Naandi Foundation at the grassroot level to ensure that the girls receive academic and material support. The objective of impacting the nation's development through education and growth of the girl child.

    Goonj

    Goonj (means an echo) a multi award winning social enterprise focused on disaster relief and other relative activities, humanitarian aid and community development to work on basic needs like clothing, sanitary pads and other needs for village while changing the architecture and dynamics of giving and receiving with dignity.

    Chetna

    CHETNA has grown to become a pioneering organisation in raising child protection issues with a team of experienced social workers and professionals. Some members of our team were themselves street and working children, who with the help of CHETNA worked their way to a better life.

    Pratham

    Pratham is an innovative learning organization created to improve the quality of education in India. Pratham focuses on high-quality, low-cost, and replicable interventions to address gaps in the education system. Providing education to children in the slums of Mumbai and has grown to wide geographical coverage.

    Sammaan

    The non-governmental organisation Sammaan Foundation has come up into establishment back in the year 2007 which ensures a better living for the people of our society extensively around the globe. They work for the unprivileged children, women and youngsters who are sadly lie below the poverty line and living in difficulties.

    Lepra Society

    LEPRA Society is a non-governmental organisation that promotes quality health care, initiates and fosters new developments and implementation. The Society aims to support the National Health Programmes in the prevention and control of diseases such as Leprosy, Lymphatic Filariasis and other neglected diseases.
